Rent-A-Pet: About Us

Experience the joy of pet companionship without long-term commitment! At Rent-A-Pet, we offer:

  • Short-term pet companionship
  • Carefully screened and healthy pets
  • Flexible rental periods
  • All supplies included
  • Professional support and guidance

Whether you're looking for a weekend cuddle buddy, a temporary hiking companion, or just want to try pet ownership before making a long-term commitment, we have the perfect pet for you!

In the Beginning ...

Rent A Pet began with a family that loved dogs. George Smith and his wife Carol owned two Irish Setters...

Advantages

  • Renters could have access to the pet at their leisure

  • Renters could pick the pet they wanted

  • Renters could exercise with the pet

  • Renters could have companionship of a pet

  • Renters would not have to pay for upkeep or medical expenses

  • The owners could have many pets and enjoy them





Disadvantages

  • The business would need to take care of housing and feeding

  • The business would need to provide medical expenses

  • The business would need to have liability insurance

  • The owners would be tied down with the care of the pets
